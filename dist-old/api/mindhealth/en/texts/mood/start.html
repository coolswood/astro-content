Daily Well-Being Check-In

Rate how you feel before and after doing CBT exercises. Regular check-ins help you notice changes over time and understand how you're progressing.

You can choose the format that works best for you. We suggest two options:

  • First, check in with yourself in the morning. During the day, do your exercises, run behavioral experiments, or work with your therapist. In the evening, rate your state again to see what has changed.
  • Or you can rate your well-being right before you start working in the app or before a session with your psychologist, and then do a second check-in immediately after you finish the exercises.

    • Both approaches are equally helpful. Choose the one that feels most natural for you.
