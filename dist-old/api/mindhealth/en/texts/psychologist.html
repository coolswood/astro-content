Connect your psychologist right in the app!

Your psychologist will be able to see your automatic thought diary entries and support you even more effectively.

All entries are automatically synced to their professional account, and you can revoke access at any time.

How to connect your psychologist:

  • Tap “Invite psychologist” and send the link to your specialist.
  • Your psychologist will accept the invitation by signing in to their professional account.
  • That’s it! You’re connected and can work together. 🎉

    • The whole process takes just a few minutes. 🕒
