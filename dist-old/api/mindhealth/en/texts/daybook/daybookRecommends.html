Our team has created a unique diary-analysis algorithm that helps you better understand your thoughts and emotions. It is trained on open data and materials based on cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT).

Below are a few tips to help improve the quality of the analysis of your entries:

Describe not only events, but also your emotions and thoughts about what happened. Remember: the more specific and detailed your entry is, the more helpful the analysis will be.

  • Try to focus on one specific situation or thought. If several important things happened during the day, it’s better to create a separate entry for each one.
  • The algorithm helps identify irrational and unhelpful beliefs, but don’t hesitate to note positive moments too — they are just as important for balance and growth.
