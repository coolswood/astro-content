Our team has developed a unique journal-analysis algorithm that helps you better understand your thoughts and reactions. The model is trained on open data and materials based on cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT).

You can describe a situation that is bothering you or simply share your thoughts in free form. The algorithm will process your text and highlight insights that can help you better understand yourself and what’s going on inside.
