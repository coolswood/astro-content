The “Approval” scale measures how strongly a person ties their self-respect to other people’s opinions. A positive score from 0 to 10 indicates that the person maintains independence, has a healthy sense of self-worth, and responds to criticism and disapproval in a balanced way.

A negative score from -10 to 0 indicates that a person is strongly dependent on how others see them. When criticized, they tend to see themselves as worthless. This emotional hypersensitivity to other people’s opinions makes them especially vulnerable to manipulation.
